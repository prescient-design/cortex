cortex.data.samplers.functional._round_robin_longest.round_robin_longest(iterables: List[SizedIterable]) → Iterable¶

Round robin of iterables until the longest have been exhausted.

Example

>>> iterables = [range(5), "ABCDE", ["cat", "dog", "rabbit"]]
>>> iterator = round_robin_longest(iterables)
>>> list(iterator)
[0, 'A', 'cat', 1, 'B', 'dog', 2, 'C', 'rabbit', 3, 'D', 'cat', 4, 'E', 'dog']
Parameters:

iterables (list[SizedIterable]) – The iterables to roundly robin

Returns:

The iterator stepping round robinly through. Cycles through shorter iterators.
